Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload?

Scroll up and click on the "Click Here To Select Files" text. Once the upload is complete, you will be redirected to a page containing the URLs to access your uploaded image(s) directly.

Are videos supported?

We will accept MP4 and WEBM videos only. We may consider adding support for more extensions if there is enough demand for it.

Are images modified?

By default, EXIF data is removed from images, if it exists. The image itself is not optimzied in any other way.

How do I share on [website]?

Unfortunately, we are not able to provide assistance on how to link your images on another website as all websites are different. You should contact the support staff of the website you want to share to for advice.

What is the maximum file size?

For all users, the filesize limit, per file, is 20 MB.

What images are supported?

We accept the following extensions: jpg, jpeg, png, gif, webm, mp4

My upload(s) were deleted?

Deleted uploads happen because of Terms Of Service violations, via a takedown request or through our automated process where uploads made anonymously are deleted because they were last viewed more than 90 days ago.

Got a question?

Feel free to contact us! We'll try and get back to you as soon as possible.
